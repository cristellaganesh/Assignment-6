About

I have always enjoyed keeping up to date with the latest fashion trends, whether that be simple fashion icons on social media or even huge events like the Met Gala! I enjoy exploring fashion design and expressing creativity through it. I like experimenting with styles, colors, and trends to create unique fashion ideas.

This hobby of mine all began with the popular colouring book: "Top Model". After begging my mum to buy me each one that was released, i slowly became obsessed with designing multiple fashion looks and surrounded myself with the wide variety of fashion trends that surfaced on social media later on in my life.

While I am still developing my personal style, I enjoy experimenting with different looks and researching the history of fashion to learn more about its evolution, while still trying to participate in the wide variety of fashion trends today.

Fashion

Alexandra Leclerc (or more widely known as Alexandra Saint Mleux) is a French fashion/lifestyle influencer aswell as an art historian. From her casual outfits strolling around the city to treating the paddock like a fashion show, she is one of my biggest fashion inspirations and someone who i really look up to especially in the fashion aspect of it.

Modern designers and influencers often draw inspiration from the confident femininity and timeless elegance of 90s Chanel. I take inspiration from the fashion line released by Chanel in the 90s as well as from their current pieces -often times it is their bag and jewellery- which i try incorporating into my everyday looks.

As a global runway model, Kendall Jenner often blends high-fashion pieces with simple everyday basics, creating looks that feel both luxurious and wearable.These are just a few examples out of many fashion icons that i look up to and take into consideration when designing/experimenting with my fashion style.

Modern fashion constantly reinterprets past styles while creating new ways for people to express individuality. Seeing how trends evolve and reappear inspires me to look deeper into the history of fashion and understand the stories behind different styles. It encourages me to develop my own perspective and appreciation for fashion as both an art form and a form of self-expression.
